What We do

We provide knowledgeable answers to your questions about cannabis, and cannabis concentrates. A lot has changed over the past few years, and finding the information that you are looking for can sometimes feel like trying to drink from a fire hose. We are a local resource to make your search for answers easier. Please allow us to share our experience and extensive database with you.  Send us an e-mail with your questions, and we will do our best to answer them, or point you in the right direction.

About Big Island Extracts

We are a small hui of like minded individuals who have come together to share our experience and techniques with the intention of one day soon providing these services to the public. Our extraction team has experience with all types of hashish production, ethanol extraction, butane, CO2, rosin tech, as well as winterization, decarboxylation, and vacuum purging. Our growing team has decades of growing experience right here on the Big Island, so we are familiar with the many challenges that one can encounter in our tropical environment. Medical cannabis and industrial hemp are our passion.

Mission

Our mission is to provide assistance to people who have questions about any of the methods of extracting concentrates from cannabis safely and comfortably. The quality and safety of your medicinal cannabis products is our top priority.
